## Do not confuse with the following issues

<CardGroup cols={2}>

    <Card title="My executions are not processing" href="/troubleshooting/executions-not-processing">
        Your application's executions are showing as 'Queued' while none are 'Running'.
    </Card>

    <Card title="My executions are stuck" href="/troubleshooting/executions-stuck">
        Your application's executions are showing as 'Running' for too long.
    </Card>

</CardGroup>

## Suggested resolutions steps

<AccordionGroup>

    <Accordion title="1. Does your application has one successful build?">

A Defer application needs a successful build to accept executions.

Double-check that your Defer application has at least one successful build. Otherwise, we won't be able to process your executions.

    </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
